Era of Genesis — 1998

The Founding Assembly

Amidst a period of significant social transition, a coalition of student intellectuals convened in Pekanbaru. Recognizing the need for an organized platform that prioritized intellectual rigor over pure activism, the foundation for the current organizational structure was drafted.

Expansion — 2005

The Wacana Journals

Pembentukan sayap publikasi resmi menandai peralihan dari diskusi internal menuju kontribusi intelektual publik. Edisi awal Buletin Garda disebarkan ke berbagai kampus di provinsi dan menguatkan reputasi organisasi sebagai ruang pemikiran.
